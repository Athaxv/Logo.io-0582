# Logo.io — Design Direction

## Brand
AI logo builder that converts hand-drawn sketches into sleek modern logo designs.

## Colors
- Background: #050505 (near-black)
- Foreground: #FFFFFF (white)
- Accent/Primary: #C8FF00 (lime/neon green-yellow)
- Muted text: #737373 / #a3a3a3
- Card/surface: #141414
- Border: #262626

## Typography
- Font: Poppins (headings + body)
- Hero heading: ~64-80px, bold, tight letter-spacing
- Body: 16px, regular
- Nav: 14-15px, medium

## Layout
- Full-width dark landing page
- Sticky navbar: logo left, links center, CTA right
- Hero: centered large heading + two CTA buttons
- Client logos bar below hero
- Decorative flower images as bg (to be added later)
- Tags/badges floating near hero

## Buttons
- Primary: lime/neon fill (#C8FF00), dark text, rounded-full
- Secondary/outline: transparent with white border, white text, rounded-full
- Nav CTA: small lime button

## Style
- Dark, modern, minimal
- Sharp contrast between dark bg and neon accent
- Generous whitespace
- Pill-shaped badges
